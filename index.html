Mission

Keeping humanity in mind.

Supporting Life

Our crew are working around the clock to make manned missions to Mars a reality. Our goal is to help progress forward with colonizing Mars and other planets to help grow the human race and find other planets with reasources and habitats we can utilize for our sustained growth.

Bio-diversity

New planets can bring new advances in medicine and health that can further the growth of humankind. We are commited to finding these resources to study, investigate and develop new treatments for the current diseases we are fighting here on Earth. We are commited to leaving planets we find and study cleaner and better than we found them.

Exploration

Our team is dedicated to exploration of the galaxy and beyond to find reasources and new ways to help mankind live longer, healthier lives. Our team has the tools and knowledge to find new breakthroughs in medicine and health while maintaining integrity to our newly found resources.

Opportunities

Only you can help save Humanity.

Space Crew

We are always in need of energetic space crew to help man our flight crews, ground staff and pilot staff.

Space Pilot

To become part of our Pilot staff will require dedication, commitment and a long road of training both physical and mental. Do you believe you have what it takes?

Research Team

Our commited research team explores newly discovered planets for wildlife, plant life and micro life forms. this role will find you travelling months at a time exploring new planets and discovering new things.

Commercial Team

We will also be offering commercial flights to our space station hotel. This role is a commercial roal, rotating shifts living and working on our space stations all over the galaxy. Meet new people and explore the galaxy while you do it.
